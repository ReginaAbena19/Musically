How Musically works

  • 1. Using the search bar search a genre
  • 2. A list of artists and their songs are displayed
  • 3. Users are then able to save their finds to their personal library.
  • 4. Users are also have the option to remove their finds from their personal library
  • 5. The user will also be able to sample a song from the artist
  • 6. View potential tour dates
